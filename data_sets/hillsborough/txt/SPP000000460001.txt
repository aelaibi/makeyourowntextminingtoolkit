        A question has arisen in relation to the alteration of the Coroner’s Certification in the case of
Kevin Williams. The original one (apparently dated 28 March 1991) gave parts of the body injured as
‘chest’ (the copy I have is poor). This seems to have been changed subsequently to ‘neck’ in a further
certificate (dated 4 April 1994?). Copy enclosed.

       It was D r Slater’s evidence at the inquest 2 & 4 May 1990 that both the neck and chest were
injured (2 May, p3) I am not aware that this was ever altered.

        Mrs Williams’ solicitors are now asserting that the only injury was to the neck and the later
certificate shows this. I wonder if you could help me with this problem. Did D r Slater ever alter his
view that asphyxia was due to the compression of the neck and chest?

         I should also be grateful if you could arrange for me to have a copy of the original certificate.

         Finally, thank you for your letter of 24 November, the points in which I have noted.
