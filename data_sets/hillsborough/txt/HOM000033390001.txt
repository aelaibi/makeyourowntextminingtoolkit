H ILLSBO RO UG H: M ERSEYSIDE PRESS COVERAG E

I attach cuttings from the above from today’s editions. I apologise for the poor
reproduction quality.

The overall coverage is less vitriolic than might have been expected, although
you (Mr Howarth) attract particular ire because of your local connections.

You (Home Secretary) may particularly want to note Trevor Hicks’
appreciation of your handling of yesterday’s meeting in his piece for the
‘Echo’, and also their somewhat scathing leader comments about your article.

You may want to consider with Ed Owen whether you wish to respond -
although as you know trying to get your voice heard in local media on this
issue is pretty much a lost battle.
