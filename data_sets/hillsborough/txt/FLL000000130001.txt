Dear Secretary,

                               Hillsborough Disaster Inquiry

     As you will be aware, the above Inquiry is being conducted
by Lord Justice Taylor.

     A preliminary hearing takes place at Sheffield Town Hall
on 28th April at 11.00a.m. the purpose of which will be to hear
applications on behalf of those who wish to be represented at
the oral hearings and to give directions as to the procedure to
be followed,

     Apart from oral evidence, Lord Justice Taylor will
consider written submissions bearing on his Inquiry.  Any club
wish anything to contribute should write to the Secretary to
the Inquiry
