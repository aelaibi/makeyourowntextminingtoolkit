                 HILLSBOROUGH CONFERENCE

            NOTE FOR COUNCILLOR PETER MILLEA


An announcement will be made by Charles Clark M.P., in the House
of Commons that Hillsborough records will be available for reference
purposes only from next week. They will be located at the Records
Office, 4th Floor, Central Library.

Access will be restricted to family members during the first two weeks
following which there will be open access to members of the public.

The records will include statements from South Yorkshire Police
Officers and evidence from the Lord Justice Taylor Inquiry.
