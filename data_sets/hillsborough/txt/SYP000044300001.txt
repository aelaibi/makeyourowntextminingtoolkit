Dear Mrs McBrien

Lord Justice Taylor has asked me to thank you
for your letters of 26 and 28 July and 2 August
with enclosures about the tragic events at
Hillsborough, and to pass on his deepest
sympathy for the loss of your son.

He is grateful for your constructive comments
and would like to assure you that your views
will be taken fully into account.
