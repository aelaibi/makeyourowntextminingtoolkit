Dear Mr Stickley

HILLSBOROUGH STADIUM DISASTER INQUIRY PART II
TECHNICAL WORKING PARTY ON THE GREEN GUIDE
I am writing to confirm the arrangements for the first meeting of
the technical working group, under the chairmanship of Professor
L Maunder.

The meeting will take place on Wednesday 18 October in Room 749
here at 50 Queen Anne's Gate, beginning with a buffet lunch
between 1.00pm - 2.00pm at which Lord Justice Taylor hopes to be
present.

Any formal agenda and papers will be circulated shortly. If you
have any queries, please do not hesitate to contact me (on
01-273-2370). Otherwise, I look forward to meeting you on 18
October.

                                       Yours sincerely




                                        JONATHAN C SWEET
                                   Secretary to the Working Party
