          MINUTES OF A DIRECTORS MEETING HELD IN THE
         CLUB BOARD ROOM ON THURSDAY 28m MARCH 1991


Hillsborough Family Support Group

The General Secretary reported on the arrangements made for the Service of
Remembrance at the Liverpool Anglican Cathedral on the 15th April 1991 in
memory of those supporters who had lost their lives in the Hillsborough
Disaster. It was agreed that wine and beer be available with the buffet meal
to be served at the Anfield Ground following the service for the families of the
deceased.
