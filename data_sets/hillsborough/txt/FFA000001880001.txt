        Dear Sir,

        F.A. CHALLENGE CUP COMPETITION 1988/89
        SEMI-FINAL TIES - SATURDAY, 15 APRIL 1989

       Further to your letter of 24 February 1989, I confirm that this
       Club would be interested in staging an F.A. Challenge Cup Semi-
       Final on Saturday, 15 April 1989 or a replay during the following
       mid-week.

       I enclose for your information a map of the ground shaving the
       different areas and capacities. You will notice that out of the
       total ground capacity of 44,010, we have seating accommodation
       for 13,597 spectators including an Executive Club enclosure which
       has 253 seats with private facilities for meals, etc., and a
       Directors's Box with a seating capacity of 124. The red line
       around three parts of the ground indicates safety fencing.

        If you require any further information, please do not hesitate
        to contact me.

       EAVID CAPPER
