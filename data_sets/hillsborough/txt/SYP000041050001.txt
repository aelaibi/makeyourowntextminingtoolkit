           COMPLAINT AGAINST MR WALTER JACKSON, ASSISTANT CHIEF CONSTABLE
           SOUTH YORKSHIRE POLICE
           Enclosed is a complaint against Mr Walter Jackson, Assistant Chief Constable,
           South Yorkshire Police made by Mr Trevor Hicks and arising out of the
           Hillsborough tragedy.
           The Police Authority at its meeting last Friday, decided that the complaint be
           investigated and that you be invited to carry out that inquiry. I have already
           been in contact with Captain Noel Taylor of the Police Complaints Authority and
           it is has been decided that he will supervise the investigation and he has also
           confirmed to me that Authority’s approval to your appointment as investigating
           officer. I should be pleased to receive your confirmation that you are prepared
           so to act.
