SUBJECT:      MANPOWER & COMMUNICATIONS HILLSBOROUGH 1989

I refer to your request for information regarding manpower and communications requirements for
the FA Cup Semi-Final football match held at the Hillsborough Stadium on Saturday 15 April 1989.

There were a total of 1,122 police officers performing duty in respect of the FA Cup Semi-Final
football match at the Hillsborough Stadium on Saturday 15 April 1989. This figure includes Traffic
personnel and policing for Sheffield city centre.

A further 261 police officers attended from all parts of the country as a result of Operation Support
at 3.10 pm.

Attached for your information at Appendixes A, B, C, D & E are parts of the match orders for
F, D and Traffic Divisions relating to Communications, a copy of pages from the radio issue book
and a schedule of manpower from F Division Match Orders.
