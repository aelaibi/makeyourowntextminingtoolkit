Dear Sir,
   My company undertakes for clients projects covering market research and
recommendations, product viability studies, identification and selection
of distributors for export markets, the analysis and remedy for sales
shortfalls and the potential for new products in new markets. These areas
of activity often apply to international and export business.

   My policy is to work exclusively for one client at a time to ensure
full availability and commitment and the completion of each project as
quickly as possible. Since my current task will be completed at the end of
April, I will then be available to take on new work.
   For the last 20 years I have been involved at senior management level
with, and handled projects for, a wide variety of consumer product and
capital goods companies including Unilever, Johnson & Johnson, Portals
Holdings, Rowntrees, Kimberley Clark and Hotpoint. The work has all been
of a sales or marketing nature usually for overseas markets.

   The minimum length of any one task would be one month.
   If you have a project for which my company might be suited, I would be
glad to visit you to discuss the matter more fully.
