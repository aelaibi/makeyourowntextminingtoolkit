Dear Dr Gold

Thank you for your letter of 3 September with enclosures from
M Cloez about the "anti-panic" fencing at Nimes.

Lord Justice Taylor has read the papers with interest and I am
writing to acknowledge receipt.  Arrangements are currently being
made for the Inquiry to visit a number of stadia both in this
country and abroad, including, it is hoped, Nimes.  Such a visit
would enable the operation of the Cloez fencing to be seen at
first hand.

The Interim Report made no recommendation on the removal of
perimeter fencing as it was felt premature to do so.  Lord
Justice Taylor has made it clear, however, that the issue will be
addressed in detail in his final report.
