Thank you very much for your letter of 23 July to Lord Justice Stuart-Smith.

As he has explained, Lord Justice Stuart-Smith is interested to hear of any possible new
evidence there might be about the Hillsborough Football Stadium disaster. He is happy to
undertake private interviews where these would help him in his work and I am sure we will
be able to arrange for interviews to take place in Liverpool.

We were interested to read that you may have new evidence, and I would be grateful if you
could let us know what this is. It would be a help if you could do so, if at all possible, by 1
September. We should then be able to make a decision about an interview (there would be
no difficulty about Mr Benton attending it).

I look forward to hearing from you. Please telephone if you wish.
