       Dear Sir

       R e : Salmon Letter

       Thank you for your letters of the 18th and 31st May.    I feel
       that   "piecemeal" disclosure of salmon letters is not really
       the way to best deal with this situation.   My own view is that
       if salmon letters are to be disclosed to other parties there
       should be mutual    disclosure - ie each party who has had a
       salmon   letter should be given copies of all   salmon  letters
       issued to other parties in turn.

       Youfts faithful 1
