Speaking to Lord Justice Taylor yesterday evening he said that he had found
the statements we had furnished in respect of the Everton -v- Norwich
Football Association Cup Semi-Final most interesting and relevant. He said
that he hoped to refer to it in his interim report. He asked, therefore,
that a report, rather than the statements, be submitted.

I have pleasure in enclosing a report from Chief Superintendent R. M.
Meller which summarises his experiences at the Villa ground on Saturday, 15
April 1989.

 I trust this is of interest to you.
