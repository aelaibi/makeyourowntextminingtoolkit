PRESENTABLE FORMAT AND MAY BE USED AS A DOCUMENT IN THE
JUDICIAL PAPERS.

THERE WILL BE OCCASIONS WHEN THE SAME OR SIMILAR
INFORMATION IS CONTAINED IN MORE THAN ONE DOCUMENT TYPE,
E.G. QUESTIONNAIRE AND A STATEMENT.
