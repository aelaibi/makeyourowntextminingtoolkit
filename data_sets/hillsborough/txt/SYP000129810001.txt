       Dear Sir,

       Annual Inspection of Sheffield Wednesday Football Stadium

       I can now confirm that the annual inspection of your Football Stadium will
       take place on Wednesday, 10th May, 1989, commencing at 10.00 a.m..  The
       inspection will include checks of the various documents, Certificates, etc.,
       that you are required to keep under the Safety Certificate Conditions.  To
       save time it might be appropriate for these to be available on the day of
       the Inspection.

       I anticipate that the inspection itself will take some time and possibly
       will extend over more than one day.

       I should like to explain that the Council has no objections to your
       representatives being present with Individual Officers when they go around
       the Stadium but I feel I should make it plain that all the Inspecting
       Officers will be properly authorised by the Council under the legislation to
       carry out inspections.

       If I can be of further help please let me know.

       Yours faithfully,




       Acting Head of Department.
