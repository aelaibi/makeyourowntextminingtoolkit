The Chief Constable, West Midlands Police, has been appointed to head the

investigation into the recent football tragedy at Hillsborough.



With immediate effect all urgent matters concerning the investigation

which need to be brought to the attention of the Investigating Officer,

can be passed by telephone to the West Midlands Police Incident

Information Centre on               between the hours of 0800 - 2000

weekends included,   (an answering machine is available out of hours) or

Fax:                                It is stressed that this Information

Centre is for urgent matters only, routine information should be kept

until a permanent Incident Room is operational.



The telephone/fax numbers are for Police use only and are not to be

released to the press or public.



Public enquiries should be made on 021-236 5000.
