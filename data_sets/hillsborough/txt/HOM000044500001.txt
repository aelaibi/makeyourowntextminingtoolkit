I am writing to inform you that the Home Secretary will be making an oral
statement to the House of Commons in the afternoon of Wednesday
18 February on the outcome of Lord Justice Stuart-Smith's scrutiny of evidence
about the Hillsborough disaster.

The Home Secretary intends to meet the families that have been involved in or
made representations to the scrutiny at a meeting at the House of Commons
immediately before the statement and you are also invited to attend this
meeting. This will take place in House of Commons Committee Room 13 at
2.15pm on the Wednesday.

I hope that you will be able to attend.
