HOUSE OF COMMONS

Mr Jim Lester (Con - Broxtowe):

        To ask the Secretary of State for the Environment, how
m any recommendations of the Popplewell Report which bear on the
responsibilities of his Department have not been implemented;
what were those recommendations; and, in each case, what were the
reasons they have not been acted upon.




MR COLIN MOYNIHAN


        None.


        In December 1986, the Home Office issued a revised version
of   the Guide to    'Safety at Sports     Grounds'.        Chapter   11 of      the
document -    'Fire Safety'    - dealt among other fire-related issues
with recommendation 10 of the Popplewell          Report.        The technical
content of the Building Regulations continues to be under review.
Later this year,        Department will issue a Consultation Document
covering all     aspects of the Building Regulations as they relate
to fire.


        The     Government's   proposals    for   a       football    membership
scheme (recommendation 15 of the Popplewell Report) are contained
in the Football Spectators Bill.
