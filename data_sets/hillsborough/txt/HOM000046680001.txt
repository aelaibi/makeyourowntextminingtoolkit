Doreen & Les Jones, parents of one of the Hillsborough victims, rang
to say that they would like the opportunity to meet LJ Stuart Smith
when he comes to Liverpool.

I said that I understood it was his intention to go to Liverpool to
meet those who wished to discuss the tragedy but that no definite
plans had yet been made. I said that we would get in touch once there
was further information on when he would visit.
