Thank you very much for your letter of 8 August. We are grateful for the points you have
made in it, and Lord Justice Stuart-Smith will take them into account.

At the end of your letter, you say that you would like to see Lord Justice Stuart-Smith to
discuss new evidence about the Hillsborough disaster. As he explained in his earlier letter to
you, the Judge is happy to undertake private interviews where these will help him in his work,
and I am sure that we will be able to arrange for these to take place in Liverpool.

Before making a decision about an interview, though, it would help if you could let me know
whether you may have other new evidence which you have not outlined in your letter.

I look forward to hearing from you (a phone call will do).
