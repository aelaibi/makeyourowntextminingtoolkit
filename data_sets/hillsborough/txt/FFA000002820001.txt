BARRY BRIGHT

FA Council Representative for Kent since 1984. Serves on Finance
and Sunday Committees for FA. Profession was as Partner with Sur­
veyors/Estate Agents before becoming Chief Executive at Gillingham
FC (Third Division) during this season. He qualified as a Class III
referee, wrote football round-ups in local newspapers for some
year and, as an administrator progressed from Junior Club Secretary
to the youngest-ever Chairman of the Kent FA in 1983. Age 41.

RONALD NOADES
FA Council Representative for The Football League since 1986.
Serves on Match & Grounds and Referees Committees of the FA. A
Company Director, he has been Chairman of Crystal Palace FC (Second
Division) since 1981. A Class II referee and FA Preliminary Badge
Coach. Previously Chairman of two other clubs - Southall (Vauxhall-
Opel League) and Wimbledon (non-League when he joined, now First
Division). Age 51.

IAN STOTT

FA Council Representative for The Football League since 1986.
Serves on the Instructional, Rules Revision and Disciplinary Comm­
ittees of the FA. Chairman and Managing Director of Oldham Athletic
FC (Second Division). Oldham is one of three League teams with an
artificial pitch. Age 55.

GEOFFREY THOMPSON

FA Council Representative for Sheffield & Hallamshire since 1979.
Serves on the Finance, Match & Grounds and Disciplinary Committees
of the FA. Disciplinary Chairman this season. Player with Chester­
field Boys, Class I referee and, as an administrator, was Assis­
tant Secretary of the Sheffield & Hallamshire FA (now Secretary)
and Secretary of the Durham FA, Birmingham FA and Doncaster Rovers
FC (now Fourth Division). Accountant and Probation Officer prior
to taking up full-time post as County Secretary. Age 43.

NOEL WHITE

FA Council Representative for Division 3 since 1976. Serves on the
Challenge Trophy, International and Disciplinary Committees of the
FA. Played four times for Cheshire FA in early 1950s. Vice-Chairman
of Altrincham FC (now GM Vauxhall Conference) for six years to
1967, including five as Secretary/Treasurer, and then became ChairmaA.
Has also been Chairman of the North-West Floodlit League and Mem­
ber of the Northern Premier League Management Committee. Now a
Director of Liverpool FC. Accountancy training. Hotel and Restaur­
ant owner. Age 59.
