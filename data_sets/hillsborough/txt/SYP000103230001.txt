F2 SUB DIVISION          ECCLESFIELD          D GROUP



SEMI-FINAL HILLSBOROUGH 15.4.89




    At 9-00 a.m. on Saturday 15 April 1989* I paraded for duty at

Ecclesfield Police Station.    Along with other members of D Group, I was

transported down by bus to the Sheffield Wednesday Football Ground at

Hillsborough.

    On arrival at Hillsborough at 9-30 a.m. we sat in the North Stand for

briefing at 10.00 a.m.

    At approximately 10.45 a.m., we patrolled as Tango 3 in a transit

van, monitoring the Stocksbridge area.    The serial consisted of an

Inspector, PS 1347 Hunt, PC 3089 Warrilow, PC 1363 Smith, PC 2041 Hone,

PC 1639 McIntosh and myself.

    We patrolled the main road leading from Oughtibridge to Wharncliffe

Side, Deepcar and Stocksbridge checking round the areas of public houses

along that route.

    At 2.45 p.m. we parked on Cockshutts Lane, Oughtibridge.     At this

time messages were being passed on the radio.    By this time we realised

 that there was some sort of problem at the ground.     As we were meant to

be meeting at Claywheels Lane at 3-15 p.m., it was decided to make our

 way towards the ground in case any assistance was needed.

     As we got closer to the ground, the radio transmission became more

 clear and I could hear someone shouting to "Open the gate".    This was

 repeated a few times.

     As the van turned left into   Leppings Lanefrom Catch Bar Lane,   I

 could see there was approximately three hundred people milling about at

 the entrance to the ground and all along Leppings Lane.
