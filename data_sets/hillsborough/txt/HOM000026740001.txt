      Dear Sir

      Please find enclosed a plan of our ground showing the pitch
      perimeter fences and the single and double gates therein.

      All fences are 2m high with the exception of that length in
      front of the South Stand terrace enclosure which is lm high.

      The capacity of each of the two large terraces is 7,000. The
      Durnsford Road end is the home end and is not in any way
      subdivided, other than by crush barriers. The Wandle terrace
      is the away end and is divided into two equal sections by a
      police channel, 1500mm wide with fences 2m high either side.

      As you will see from the plan, the fence in front of the
      Durnsford Road terrace has six single gates in it and the
      fence in front of the Wandle terrace has one double and one
      single gate.

      Only the double gate in the Wandle terrace fence was installed
      as an emergency exit. All other gates were installed for the
      assistance of stewards and police - although these days they
      have come to be regarded as emergency exit gates.

      I hope thisinformation is of interest to you.                                                  Please contact
      me again if you require any further assistance.
