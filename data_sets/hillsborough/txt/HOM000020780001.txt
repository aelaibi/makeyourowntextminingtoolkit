HILLSBOROUGH STADIUM DISASTER INQUIRY PART II
TECHNICAL WORKING PARTY ON THE GREEN GUIDE


I am very pleased that you are able to accept Lord Justice
Taylor's invitation to sit on a small technical working party on
the Guide to Safety at Sports Grounds. The terms of reference
and membership of the working party are attached.

The Chairman of the working party is Professor Leonard Maunder,
one of Lord Justice Taylor's Assessors on the Hillsborough
Stadium Disaster Inquiry.

The Secretary is Jonathan Sweet, my assistant as Secretary to the
Inquiry. He will be contacting you shortly about the date for
the first meeting of the working party.




                                        R M WHALLEY
                                   Secretary to the Inquiry
TERMS OF REFERENCE



     To review technical aspects of the Guide to Safety at
     Sports Grounds; identify areas needing clarification
     or amendment; and recommend accordingly.
