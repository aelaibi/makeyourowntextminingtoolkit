    A meeting of the South Yorkshire Police Authority will be held on Friday 11 May

    Members are reminded that it is a requirement of the Conference Centre that car
    parking at the Centre is by means of a ticket (available from the Reception Desk

    AGENDA

    1.     Urgent Items. To determine whether there are any additional items of
            business which by reason of special circumstances the Chairman is of the
            opinion should be considered at this meeting (the reasons for such urgency
            to be stated).
    2.      Exclusion of Public and Press. To identify items where resolutions may be
            moved to exclude the public and press. (For items marked * the public and
            press may be excluded from the meeting).
    3.     Police Authority Certificate of Commendation. On 30 March 1990, the
           Authority resolved to award its Certificate of Commendation to Mr P D Hirst
           of Sheffield in recognition of his determined and public spirited actions
           on 5 May 1989, resulting in a perpetrator of a crime having been brought to
           justice. Mr Hirst has indicated that he will be able to be present at this
           meeting of the Authority to receive the Certificate of Commendation.
