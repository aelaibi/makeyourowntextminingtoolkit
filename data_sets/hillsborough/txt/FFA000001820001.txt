*   Dear Mr. Clark,

    F.A. Challenge Cup - Season 1 988-89
    Semi-Final Tie - 15th April 1989

    Thank you for your letter dated 24thFebruary I9 8 9 , we should
    be pleased to stage a Semi-Final Tie on Saturday 15th April
    1989 if invited to do so by your Association.

    Our Club does have the necessary insurance cover for such a
    match and we have pleasure in enclosing a plan showing the various
    areas and the capacity of separate standing compartments. Three
    sides of the ground has safety fencing erected the part that
    doesn't is that in front of the 1222Paddock seating        area.
    The total capacity of the ground as per the Safety Certificate
    is 38,408.

    We have an Executive Club with Restaurant licensed facilities
    which also has a 'bar'.   The Executive Club special seating
    in the stand has 161 seats, and the Directors Guest Room which
    also has a 'bar' is used by guests using the Directors Box seats.
    We also have a small 'Ladies' room. We understand the provision
    regarding persons using Executive Boxes of which we have 25
    plus a Sponsors Box seating 16 persons which is not let on a
    Season basis. (The Directors Box seats 80 persons).

    We trust that the foregoing meets with your satisfaction.



    Yours sincerely
