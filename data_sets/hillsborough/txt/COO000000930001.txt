   Thank you for your recent letter to the Prime Minister about
the disaster at Hillsborough.       I have been asked to reply.


    You wrote as an eye witness and long time football supporter.
I am sure that your thoughtful comments and suggestions will
prove useful to the Inquiry which has been set up under the
Chairmanship of Lord Justice Taylor to investigate the events of
Hillsborough and to make recommendations.               I have therefore
forwarded your letter to Mr R Whalley, Secretary to the Enquiry,
who can be contacted at this address.




                                               MISS C J BANNISTER
