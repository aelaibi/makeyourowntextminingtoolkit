                                                   24th April,   1989




Dear

                  has passed to me a copy of your letter dated 16th
April with the detailed account of your boys' visit to the
semi-final at Sheffield Wednesday F. C.

   I am grateful to you for this detailed information which will be
very helpful in the total picture we are endeavouring to compile.
If there is a need to obtain further details from your sons we will
contact you.

   I am sure    I      ^   Ihas by now written to you personally and
has conveyed to you our share in your personal joy that your sons
survived this tragedy, although I am sure they will not forget it.

   With best wishes.

                                Yours sincerely,




                              Administration Manager


b. c. c. Mr. R. H. G. Kelly
