                                  PRESS RELEASE



The PoJice Complaints Authority today announced that the Investigation which it
supervised into complaints against some police officers of the South       :
following the tragedy at the Hillsborough football stadium in Sheffield has been
completed to its satisfaction.

The Investigating Officer whose appointment was approved by the Authority was Mr
Leslie Sharp, Chief Constable of Cumbria Constabulary and he reported to Captain Noel
Taylor, the Police Complaints Authority's supervising Member.

The overall investigation has examined 9560 questionnaires, 53«fl statements, 2392 sets
of documents including 71 hours of video recordings, 2411 photographs and the
transcripts of .the lib persons who gave evidence to the Lord Justice Taylor enquiry.

The seven persons who made complaints against a total of seven police officers have
been written to by the Authority advising them that the investigation has been
