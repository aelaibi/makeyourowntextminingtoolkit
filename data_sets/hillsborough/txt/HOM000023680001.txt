PARLIAMENTARY QUESTION FOR WRITTEN ANSWER ON 15 DECEMBER 1989



Mr Michael Colvin (Romsey and Waterside): To ask the Secretary
of State for the Home Department, when he expects the report by
Lord Justice Taylor on crowd control and safety at sports stadia
to be completed.




DRAFT REPLY



The timing of the Final Report by the Inquiry is a matter for

Lord Justice Taylor.   I understand that he hopes to complete his
report in January 1990.
