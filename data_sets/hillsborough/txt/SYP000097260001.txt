 I refer to Lord Justice Taylor's Inquiry into the events at Hillsborough
 Football Ground, Sheffield, on Saturday 15 April 1989.

 Hammond Suddards, Solicitors, of Empire House, 10 Piccadilly, Bradford, acting
 for the South Yorkshire Police, have instructed that we request the West
 Midlands Police Enquiry Team to supply copies of any Criminal Justice Act
 statements taken from non-South Yorkshire Police officers. The provision of
 such statements would greatly assist my officers who are in the process of
 adducing evidence on my behalf to present to the Inquiry. Theattached
 schedule is a list of non-South Yorkshire Police officers whogave assistance
 at Hillsborough on 15 April 1989.

 Your assistance in this matter is    greatly appreciated.
