Dear

     May I thank you for your kind letter regarding
the tragedy at Hillsborough.

     I am touched by your sympathy and concern for
all those involved, particularly the bereaved.

      As you may be aware, Lord Justice Taylor is
conducting an enquiry into the events at Hillsborough,
and I have no doubt that his report will make
reconflnendation to prevent such an awful tragedy occurring
again.

                     Yours sincerely,




                        J. COLLEDGE
                   Chief Superintendent




Guernsey.
