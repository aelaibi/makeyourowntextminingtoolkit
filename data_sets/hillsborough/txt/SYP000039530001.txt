            Dear

            I am in receipt of your letter of 5 September 1989 which talks about our
            involvement in the Hillsborough disaster. I have noted the contents of
            your letter and have sent a copy of it to the Secretary to the Inquiry
            who, no doubt, will draw it to the attention of Lord Justice Taylor.

            I have also noted the comments made by your husband on the reverse of
            your letter.

           May I take this opportunity of thanking you for taking the trouble to
           write and express your opinion. Your continued support for law and
           order is appreciated, regardless of who may be considered blameworthy.

            Yours sincerely


                                                                         Q.




           Assistant Chief Constable
           (Hillsborough Inquiry)
