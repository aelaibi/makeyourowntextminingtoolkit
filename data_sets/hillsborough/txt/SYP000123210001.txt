                                          Divisional Commander,
                                          Sir,

                                               I have to report that on Saturday 15th April, 1989, I
                                          was off duty when I attended the Hillsborough Stadium,
                                          Sheffield, in order to watch the F.A. Cup Semi-Final between
                                          Liverpool and Nottingham Forest football clubs.

                                               I stood on the Spion Kop area of Hillsborough Stadium
                                          and throughout the incident that followed I remained in the
                                          same position.

                                               I submit this report as a result of a teleprinter message
                                          No. 0206 of 17th April, 1989, received from the Chief Constable,
                                          Merseyside Police.
