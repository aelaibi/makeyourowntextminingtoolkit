Dear

Thankyou for your letter of 7 February to the Home Secretary
regarding Lord Justice   Taylor's Report   into the Hillsborough
Stadium disaster.  I apologise for the   delay in replying.

Following  the publication   of the report   the   Home Secretary
made a statement     in the House of       Commons outlining   the
Government's      response    to      Lord     Justice    Taylor's
recommendations.    I enclose    a copy   of that statement.    In
addition the Chancellor announced, in his budget last year,
that  the Government are making available        £100 million   to
football clubs   through the Football Trust to assist them in
improving ther grounds.

I hope this information is of use to you.
