I would just like to bring your attention to a frightening experience I
had at Wembley Stadium on Sunday, 9 April, the Littlewoods Cup Final.
My feelings were put down in a letter of 10 April to the Managing Director
of Wembley, Mr Alan Coppin, a copy of which is enclosed.

In view of this weekends' disaster, I think you will find its contents
extremely disturbing.

Yours faithfully
