         Dear

         Thank you for your recent letter to Mr Moynihan
         which has been passed to this department in view of our
         responsibilities for the Safety of Sports Grounds Act. I
         apologise for the delay in replying to you.

         You express your concern that the recommendations contained in
         the Final Report of Lord Justice Taylor's inquiry into the
         ^illsborough Stadium disaster should not be applied to sports
         other than football and in particular Stock Car Racing.

         The Report states that "crowd problems are directly related to
         the nature of the event and the numbers attending it." In
         circulating the Report to local authorities the Home Office
         advised that it would be consistent with the Report's findings
         for local authorities to take proper account of these factors
         when applying the recommendations to individual grounds.

                                                                     Yours sincerely




                                                                    MR S BILBE
                                                                    (Fire and Emergency
                                                                    Planning Department)
