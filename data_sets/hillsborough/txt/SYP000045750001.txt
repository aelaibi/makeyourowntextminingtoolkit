            Dear Mr Daniels

            Thank you for your letter of 25   January 1990 in which you make extremely
            complimentary remarks about the   efforts that my officers have made in
            respect of their enquiries into   the Hillsborough disaster. I appreciate
            your comments and I have passed   them on to the officers concerned.

            In respect of your ’strange* request it is not possible, at this time,
            to release any photographs as we are continuing the criminal and coronal
            investigations. I cannot, therefore, accede to your request. However,
            I have noted your request and will review it after the criminal and
            coronal enquiries have been completed.            ^ ^

            Yours sincerely




            Assistant Chief Constable
