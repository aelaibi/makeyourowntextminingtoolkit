I am writing briefly to acknowledge, with thanks, your helpful letter of 7 January. I have
passed it to Lord Justice Stuart-Smith.

There is one word in your letter that we cannot make out. I have marked it in pink on the
attached copy - could you let please me know what it is? (a phone message would be fine).

When we spoke over the phone the other day you mentioned another officer who might help,
who was on a course in London. I tried to track him down but could not do so. I would be
happy to hear from him direct, but that will need to be soon as time for the scrutiny is ru n n in g
short.




C F BONE
Secretary to the Scrutiny
