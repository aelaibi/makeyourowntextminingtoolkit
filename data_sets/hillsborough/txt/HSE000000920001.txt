HILLSBOROUGH INVESTIGATION - MEDIA INTEREST


In the last few days I have had several discussions with
HSE Press Office (Bootle), about press coverage of the HSE's
contribution to the Hillsborough Investigation. Here is a summary of the
current position:

1) Several TV companies have expressed an interest in making a
documentary on the investigation and they would welcome the opportunity
of filming in the Laboratory. This could be done initially without the
disclosure of our results and interviews could be carried out later.
Although these documentaries may not be broadcast until next year, Ms
       fears the HSE may "miss the boat" if a decision on this is not
forthcoming in the next few days. In our discussions July 6 &/or 7
emerged as possible days on which filming could take place.

2) There is considerable press and media interest in HSE's results which
have now be referred to openly at the Inquiry and have been accepted
apparently by all the Q Cs. The Inquiry Press Officer has intimated that
an interview with an HSE spokesman would be welcomed, possibly combined
with the opportunity of photographing the hardware at the Laboratory.
