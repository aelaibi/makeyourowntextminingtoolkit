Dear Sir,


Re:   Graham John Roberts - Number 79 - killed at Hillsborough


Since our previous letters of 16 July and 7 August we have
received PC A T Wadsworth's statement - copy enclosed.   This is
new evidence for us after eight years and six months.   He states
that Graham was treated because he had a pulse and was put on a
saline drip.   This also leads us to believe that Graham's
postmortem w as not carried out thoroughly enough as it failed to
mention this


According to Chief Constable R Wells in his letter to us dated 17
January 1992 - copy enclosed - we believe this evidence was
suppressed because it showed that people were alive and could
have been saved after 3.15pm.

What this new evidence shows is the inadequacy of the inquest
into the circumstances of Graham's death and the denial of our
right to cross-examine all available evidence.  We consider that
we are owed an explanation of this serious omission and believe
that justice requires that the verdicts of Accidental Death be
quashed.

We request a meeting with you to discuss this new evidence.

Yours sincerely,




Mr S Roberts         Mrs D J Roberts         Miss S E Roberts
Graham's father      G r a h a m 's mother   Graham's sister

          I am very very sorry that you lost Graham at Hillsborough and wish to goodness
          that there were some way of turning the clock back.

          Your letter is constructive and looks to the future, which is very welcome, but your
          grief is obvious and, so far as I can, I join you in it.

      5 As you concede, some good work was done on the day by many of our officers.
           If I read the extract correctly, PC Best saw that Graham was breathing and turned
      '■’i to others more in need of help. That judgement may have proved to have been
         i wrong but I can imagine - just - the circumstances under which it was made.     In
        { your cutting, the 70 year old pensioner had stopped breathing and Constable
       *1              R ightly sprang into action.

          On just the limited information I have, the cases seem quite different. But in the
