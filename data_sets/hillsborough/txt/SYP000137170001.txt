D EPU TY C H IE F CONSTABLE’S MEMORANDUM
Sir,

With regard to the query raised by Mrs McBRIEN:-

Attached is the Sporting Events (Control of Alcohol Etc) Act 1985, and two aide
memoirs issued to West Midlands Police Officers.

West Midlands Police have no specific policy in relation to this.

There was a recommendation by the Traffic Commissioners (which is not
enforceable), which states that coaches should not arrive at the planned event
more than one hour before kick-off.   However, since the Hillsborough Stadium
disaster, tihis has been altered to read that coaches should not arrive more
than two hours before kick-off but less than one hour before the commencement
of the game.

The above is recorded in the Traffic Commissioners Guidelines which are issued
to all bus/coach operations (copy not held by West Midlands Police).




20th November, 1990
