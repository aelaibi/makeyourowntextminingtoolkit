                  SUBJECT:     F.A. CUP SEMI-FINAL, HILLSBOROUGH - 15.4.89



                  The attached pro-forma is forwarded for your information
                  and attention.    Could it be photostated in-house and
                  distributed to all officers under your command, in order
                  that it can be established which officers were involved in
                  incidents concerning the Semi-Final Football Match at
                  Hillsborough on the 15 April 1989.        Nil returns are
                  required and could the completed forms be forwarded to the
                  Incident Room, Hammerton Road Police Station by the 18
                  April 1989.

                  Officers on Annual Leave should complete the questionnaire
                  immediately on their return to duty.




                                     +- 1
