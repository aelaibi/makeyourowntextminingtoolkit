        Dear

        May I, on behalf of the Chief Constable, acknowledge receipt of
        your letter of 18 September.

        As you may be aware a criminal enquiry is being conducted by the
        Chief Constable of the West Midlands Police, Mr Geoffrey Dear,
        into matters arising from the Hillsborough tragedy.  It will be
        for Mr Dear, at the conclusion of the enquiry, to recommend to
        the Director of Public Prosecutions whether any criminal
        proceedings should be brought.

        I trust that this information may be of assistance to you.

        Yours sincerely
