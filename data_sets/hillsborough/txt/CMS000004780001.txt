     football grounds.

     I quite understand                   concern about the cost of
     providing all seater accommodation at football league grounds and
     his anxiety that it will not change the attitude of some of those
     who go to football matches.    The Final Report by Lord Justice
     Taylor nevertheless presents a depressing picture of professional
     football. The Report points to the link between the quality of
     accommodation, crowd safety and crowd behaviour - which suffers
     when spectators are accommodated in poor, sometimes squalid,
     conditions.

     Policing of football grounds is, as recognised by
     an important element in achieving safety. Indeed major football
     matches would not be able to take place without proper policing.
     Spectator safety is however a more complex matter and the police
     were not the only ones to be criticised by the Inquiry. We must
     now look to the future and all seated accommodation is identified
     by Lord Justice Taylor as the, one move that can do more to
     achieve improvements in safety^crowd control and crowd behaviour
Q)   than any other single measure. The Government agrees that such
     a move is a major step towards creating a safer, better
     environment in which spectators can enjoy the game. That is why
     we have supported Lord Justice Taylor's recommendation and intend
     to implement it.
G>
     Responsibility for the safety and comfort of their customers
     rests with the football clubs themselves. Financial assistance
     is available to them. For example the Football Trust anticipates
     £75 million to be made available to the professional game over
     the next 10 years. This is in addition to income available from
     television rights and the pools companies, currently amounting
     to almost £26 million each year. It is for the clubs therefore
     to decide whether they can, or should, increase the price of
     admission in order to help meet the cost of long overdue ground
     ’mprovements.
